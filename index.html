Severe Criteria

A Severe thunderstorm is defined as meeting one or more of the following:

  • Wind: Gusts of 50 knots (58 mph) or greater.
  • Hail: 2.0 cm (approx 0.75 inch) diameter or larger.
  • Tornadoes: Any tornado.
  • Flooding: Extreme rainfall rates leading to flash flooding.
